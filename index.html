About the Project

Pyrovia is developing a cutting-edge pyrolysis plant located in Trenčianske Bohuslavice, Slovakia, dedicated to the sustainable processing of plastic waste that contains no PVC. The plant leverages low-temperature pyrolysis technology to thermally decompose plastics and convert them into valuable by-products such as synthetic oil, industrial carbon (char), and syngas.

Designed for continuous daily operation, the plant will be capable of processing 100 tons of waste per day, while maximizing energy recovery through internal reuse of the produced gas.

Technology Benefits

  • Low-temperature pyrolysis (400–600°C) – no incineration
  • Energy-efficient and environmentally friendly
  • Production of valuable outputs: oil, gas, carbon
  • Self-sustained electricity generation using syngas
  • Designed for industrial-scale deployment
